Ishidoro Zen Gardens Inc. specializes in creating authentic and affordable Japanese style gardens that enhance the beauty and tranquility of indoor and outdoor spaces in Calgary. Whether you are a working professional, retiree, or stay-at-home parent, we are dedicated to bringing your vision to life with our expert design, construction, and maintenance services.

Our Company's Origins and Commitment to Calgary

Started 08 June 2023, our roots trace back to a family legacy of construction and a passion for craftsmanship. In the 1980s, the founder's father began a construction business in Puerto La Cruz (ANZ) Venezuela. He thrived thanks to his dedication to quality and customer satisfaction. Building on this heritage, the youngest son relocated to Calgary (AB) Canada in May 2009 with a vision to excel in landscaping services.
