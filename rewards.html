Loyalty Reward Program

This program is designed to reward our valued customers for their continued patronage; and to show our appreciation for their loyalty.

How it Works

For every dollar spent on our services, the customer will earn one point. Our goal is to encourage customers to continue to use our services and to recommend us to their friends and family.

Terms and Conditions

To be eligible for the loyalty reward program, the new or referred person must hire our services.

1. The new customer's account must be current, with no outstanding balances.

2. Points are non-transferable and have no cash value.

3. Points can only be redeemed for discounts on future services.

4. Points cannot be redeemed for cash or used to pay for past services.

5. Points cannot be combined with other discounts or promotions.

6. The company reserves the right to modify or terminate the loyalty reward program.

Referral Reward Program

This program is designed to reward our loyal customers for referring new customers, family and friends to our company.

How it Works

For every new customer that you refer to us and who hires our services, you will earn a 5% discount. There is no cap or limit to the number of referrals that you may make and claim.

Terms and Conditions

To be eligible for the referral reward program, the new or referred person must hire our services.

1. The new customer must mention your name at the time of hiring our services.

2. Points are non-transferable and have no cash value.

3. Points can only be redeemed for discounts on future services.

4. Points cannot be redeemed for cash or used to pay for past services.

5. Points cannot be combined with other discounts or promotions.

6. The company reserves the right to modify or terminate the referral reward program.
