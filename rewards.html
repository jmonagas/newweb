Rewards Programs

Our rewards programs are designed to show appreciation for our valued customers' ongoing support, loyalty, and referrals to our company.

1. Eligibility: To qualify, new or referred customers must hire our services, and mention your name at the time of engagement.

2. Validity: Customers must maintain a current account with no outstanding balances to earn and redeem points.

3. Value: Points are non-transferable and have no cash value.

4. Usage: Points can only be redeemed for discounts on future services, and cannot be exchanged for cash or used to settle previous balances.

5. Pooling: Points cannot be combined with other discounts or promotions.

6. Authority: The company reserves the right to modify or terminate either of the rewards programs.
