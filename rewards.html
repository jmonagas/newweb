Ishidoro Zen Gardens' Loyalty and Referral Rewards

With a Deep Respect of Zen Philosophy

Loyalty Rewards Program

This program is designed to reward our valued customers for their continued patronage; and to show our appreciation for their loyalty.

How it Works

For every dollar spent on our services, the customer will earn one point. Our goal is to encourage customers to continue to use our services and to recommend us to their friends and family.

Terms and Conditions

To be eligible for the loyalty rewards program, the new or referred person must hire our services.

  1. The new customer's account must be current, with no outstanding balances.
  2. Points are non-transferable and have no cash value.
  3. Points can only be redeemed for discounts on future services.
  4. Points cannot be redeemed for cash or used to pay for past services.
  5. Points cannot be combined with other discounts or promotions.
  6. The company reserves the right to modify or terminate the loyalty rewards program.

Referral Rewards Program

This program is designed to reward our loyal customers for referring new customers, family and friends to our company.

How it Works

For every new customer that you refer to us and who hires our services, you will earn a 5% discount. There is no cap or limit to the number of referrals that you may make and claim.

Terms and Conditions

To be eligible for the referral rewards program, the new or referred person must hire our services.

  1. The new customer must mention your name at the time of hiring our services.
  2. Points are non-transferable and have no cash value.
  3. Points can only be redeemed for discounts on future services.
  4. Points cannot be redeemed for cash or used to pay for past services.
  5. Points cannot be combined with other discounts or promotions.
  6. The company reserves the right to modify or terminate the referral rewards program.

Customer Satisfaction and Trust

Are the Keys to Our Company's Success

  1. You can sign up for the loyalty rewards program by contacting Ishidoro Zen Gardens directly. We will be happy to assist you with the registration process.
  2. To check your points balance, please contact Ishidoro Zen Gardens directly. We will be able to provide you with your current points balance and any other information you may need about the loyalty or referral rewards program.
  3. To redeem your points, please contact Ishidoro Zen Gardens directly. We will be able to assist you with the redemption process and answer any questions you may have.
