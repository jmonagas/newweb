To promote a fair and transparent rewards experience for all our valued clients, we have established the following guidelines:

🞇  Account Status: To earn and redeem points, maintain a current account with no outstanding balances.

🞇  Point Value: Points are non-transferable and have no cash value.

🞇  Redemption: Points may only be used for discounts on future services, and cannot be exchanged for cash or applied to previous balances.
