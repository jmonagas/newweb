Why We Love Our Work

Our mission is to elevate our clients' outdoor living with gardens that merge the artistry of Japanese landscaping with their personal vision. Starting June 8, 2023, Ishidoro Zen Gardens has dedicated itself to creating serene outdoor spaces that blend natural beauty with the timeless elegance of Japanese aesthetics. We specialize in transforming ordinary yards into peaceful retreats where you can unwind while enjoying a placid atmosphere. Our expertise lies in fusing Zen principles with nature's splendor, enhancing homes and businesses throughout the Greater Calgary, bringing a touch of tranquility and charm to our communities with every project.

How We Get Things Done

Our job is more than just landscaping: It's about crafting spaces where you can escape the hustle and enjoy moments of detachment and reflection. Picture a collaborative journey where your dream garden takes shape. Our process begins with a personalized consultation to dive deep into your vision. We listen, we understand, and then we design — creating a modern Japanese garden that reflects your unique style and spirit.

Our designs translate your ideas into a stunning, customized garden plan. Whether it's a tranquil Zen corner, a calming water feature, or a cozy tea house, each feature or element is meant to enhance your lifestyle and your property.

The transformation doesn't stop at design. Our expert landscaping meticulously brings your vision to life. From the initial groundwork to the final placement of every rock or plant, every detail is managed with precision to create a Japanese garden that's both breathtaking and functional.

Our commitment extends beyond the installation. With our comprehensive maintenance packages, your garden remains immaculate throughout the seasons. We handle everything: From regular pruning and weeding to seasonal clean-ups; ensuring your garden is always a source of joy and relaxation.
