Typical Questions We Get in Consultations

⯈  Is there a fee for the initial consultation?

We offer two options to start your journey: (a.) a complimentary 15-minute phone consultation to discuss your vision, and (b.) a full email consultation for detailed insights. If you prefer an on-site visit, we charge a nominal fee of $150, which will be credited towards your project should you choose to proceed with our services.

⯈  What is the typical timeline for a Japanese garden project in Calgary?

Project timelines can vary significantly based on the scope and complexity of your design. A small Zen garden may be completed in just a week, while a comprehensive property transformation could take several weeks to months. We always account for Calgary's weather patterns when planning your project timeline.

⯈  Can you work with my existing landscape?

Absolutely! We specialize in integrating Japanese garden elements into your existing landscape, ensuring a harmonious blend of new features with your current plants and structures.

⯈  Do you offer maintenance services for gardens you didn't install?

Yes, we provide expert maintenance services for all Japanese-inspired gardens, regardless of who installed them. We'll assess your garden and create a customized maintenance plan to keep it thriving.

⯈  What specific areas do you serve?

We primarily serve Calgary and the surrounding areas, including Airdrie, Chestermere, Okotoks, High River, and Cochrane. If you're outside of these areas, please contact us to discuss your project.

⯈  How much does a Japanese garden cost?

The cost of your Japanese garden will depend on various factors, including the size of your space, design complexity, and materials used. During our complimentary consultation, we can provide a more accurate estimate tailored to your specific needs and budget.

⯈  How long does it take to create a Japanese garden?

The timeline can vary from a few weeks for smaller projects to several months for larger, more complex landscapes. We'll provide a detailed timeline during our consultation process.

⯈  Do you offer other landscaping maintenance services?

We offer comprehensive maintenance services designed to keep your Japanese garden looking beautiful year-round. We can discuss additional maintenance options during your consultation to suit your garden's unique needs.

⯈  Why do you call yourselves: Calgary's Own Japanese Landscaping Company?

Ishidoro Zen Gardens Inc. offers authentic and affordable Japanese and Zen style landscaping services in Calgary, AB. We specialize in Japanese garden design and construction for homes and condominiums, focusing on creating serene and practical environments.

⯈  Are you Japanese?

No, we are Canadian-Venezuelan. Our expertise lies in designing, building, and maintaining Japanese and Zen gardens, rooted in a deep understanding of Japanese culture and Zen philosophy. Inspired by historical manuals like the Sakuteiki, we emphasize balance, harmony, and simplicity in every project.

⯈  Are you Landscape Architects?

In Alberta, the title "Landscape Architect" is a protected designation under (a.) the Professional and Occupational Associations Registration Act, and (b.) the Landscape Architects Regulation. To officially use this title, one must register as a member of the Alberta Association of Landscape Architects (AALA). We have extensive landscaping expertise but have no plans to pursue this legal designation.

⯈  What Makes a Garden a Japanese Garden?

A Japanese garden is defined not by its geographical origin but by its adherence to timeless design principles. The thoughtful integration of natural elements creates a unique space that embodies tranquility and beauty.

⯈  What guarantee do you offer?

We provide a one-year limited warranty on our workmanship, ensuring peace of mind in your investment. Specific terms, coverage details, and claims history remain confidential, as they are utilized solely to fulfill our obligations. Warranties for materials, plants, and related items are governed by our suppliers' policies.

⯈  What are your company's origins?

Founded on June 8, 2023, our company has deep roots in a family legacy of construction and craftsmanship. The founder's father began a successful construction business in Puerto La Cruz (ANZ), Venezuela, in the 1980s, driven by a commitment to quality and customer satisfaction. Building on this heritage, the youngest son relocated to Calgary in May 2009 with a vision to excel in landscaping services.

⯈  What is your professional background?

The founder of Ishidoro Zen Gardens Inc. brings a wealth of experience in three business sectors: Construction, landscaping, and property management, ensuring a comprehensive approach to every project we undertake.
