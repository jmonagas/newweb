Our Company Roots and Story

In our experience, Japanese style gardening and Zen landscaping is a continuous and incremental work of the elements that adds up to a substantial physical and psychological change over time.Although incorporation took place in Calgary in June 2023, our roots go a long way back to the 1980s. The founder's father started and managed a small construction company in Puerto La Cruz (State of Anzoategui) Venezuela. Passion and dedication run in the family.
