Our Company Roots and Story

Japanese Zen style landscaping is a continuous and incremental work of the elements that adds up to a substantial physical and psychological change over time. The founder's father started and managed a small construction company back in the 1980s in Puerto La Cruz (ANZ) Venezuela. Passion and dedication run in the family. So, the founder relocated to Calgary in the 2000s to build his own world class construction and consulting company, with an aim at providing professional services in whatever combination is required to meet our clients' needs.

A dry garden requires less care and attention than a traditional lawn; which is just perfect for those homeowners who have limited time and a budget for their upkeeping. This can transform any space into a peaceful retreat within their own homes. Our customers are real estate owners who value aesthetics, tranquility, and sustainability in their outdoor spaces. They usually are busy professionals who want a low maintenance garden, retirees who enjoy gardening as a hobby, or families who want a safe and fun place.
