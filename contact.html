Our Company's Origins and History

To Help You Make an Informed Decision

Japanese Zen style landscaping is a continuous and incremental work of the elements that adds up to a substantial physical and psychological change over time. The founder's father started a small construction company in Puerto La Cruz (ANZ) Venezuela in the 1980s. He managed the company with his two sons, working on various projects for private and public clients. A passion for construction runs in the family. So when the founder relocated to Calgary in May 2009, he aimed to build his own world-class construction and consulting company. His goal was to provide professional services tailored to meet each client's needs.

We are proud to be a part of the Calgary community. We actively participate in local events, contribute to community development initiatives, and reinforce our commitment to the city we serve. We regularly share updates about our projects, offer tips, and engage with our customers and followers online. This allows us to reach a wider audience and interact with the Greater Calgary area. As more people seek peaceful retreats in their homes and communities, demand for Japanese style gardening and Zen landscaping services will likely rise.

A dry garden requires less care and attention than a traditional lawn; which is just perfect for busy homeowners with limited time and gardening budgets. This low-maintenance landscaping can transform any outdoor space into a peaceful retreat. Our customers are real estate owners who value aesthetics, tranquility, and sustainability for their properties. They tend to be busy professionals seeking hassle-free gardens, retirees who enjoy gardening as a hobby, or families wanting a safe, enjoyable outdoor area.

Despite what you may see online these days, Japanese and Zen style gardens have no religious purpose. They can be for personal growth or simply enjoyment. Disregard those misleading ads or videos promising unrealistic benefits from a Japanese style garden. Instead, ask yourself: what meaning does this hold for me personally? From our initial discussion onwards, we will make sure your vision for your ideal garden comes to life. We'll take all feedback seriously, using both positive and negative comments to continuously improve our services.
