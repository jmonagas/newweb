Creating a Japanese Style Garden on a Budget in Calgary

By Jose G. Monagas, December 2023

If you are looking to boost your home's appeal and value, consider incorporating a Japanese Zen style garden or landscape a profitable choice — as the average house price in Calgary was expected to hit $564,000 by 31 December 2023.

For this purpose, you might need the assistance of a landscape architect as they can offer guidance on plant selection, layout, and structures. Their services typically cost between $2,000 and $6,000. Next, to direct the landscaping crews during construction, a garden design plan is necessary. The cost can range from $2,000 to $6,000.

The selection of plants and trees plays a vital role in designing a Japanese style garden and landscape. The cost can range from $5,000 to $30,000, depending on the species and quantities you chose. It is advisable to consult with a local nursery or horticultural expert, especially considering that some species may not be suitable for Calgary's climate.

For example, you can diversify your plant collection by adding: 10 types of lilacs for their fragrant blooms in spring, 10 varieties of maples known for their stunning fall colors, 10 hardy bushes perfect for Calgary gardens, 10 species of pines for greenery all year round, and 10 caragana species that thrive in harsh climates.

Rocks and gravel play a crucial role in Japanese Zen style gardens. Prices for these elements typically range from $2,000 to $6,000. Incorporating permanent fixtures like stone lanterns, entrance gates, and fences can enhance the traditional feel of your Japanese style garden. These fixtures may cost anywhere from $5,000 to $20,000. Additionally, transportation and delivery expenses for materials to your location can vary from $2,000 to $6,000.

The costs associated with landscaping crews can vary significantly, depending on the complexity of your garden design and size. These expenses can range from $10,000 to $50,000. Next, if you do not know yet or rather prefer not to maintain your Japanese style garden on your own, then it is recommended to budget $1,000 to $6,000 for yearly maintenance and upkeep.

There Are Several Other Factors to Consider When Budgeting for Your Japanese Garden

You might have to get a few City of Calgary's permits depending on the size of your project and the regulations in your community. The cost of these permits can differ. If your garden design involves water features or outdoor lighting, you should also take into account the utility costs, unless you opt for solar powered features.

Make sure to set aside some funds for replacing plants or protecting them during the winter months. Also, consider budgeting for pest control to keep your garden healthy and thriving.

Lastly, if you have not much experience in gardening, it is a good idea to hire a professional to take care of regular maintenance tasks such as pruning, shaping, and weeding. Especially if your garden has valuable structures or rare plants, it might be worth considering getting them insured.
