Standard Business Procedure for a Great Building Experience

Our primary objective is to optimize business operations by ensuring efficiency, maintaining work quality, promoting uniformity, and achieving top performance. We prioritize minimizing miscommunication and non-compliance with regulations.

Try Our Straightforward Process Today

By appointment only, we offer free consultations and estimates tailored to families and budgets, ensuring a friendly experience. Our one-year warranty backs our high-quality workmanship. Whether for indoor or outdoor spaces, our customized gardens provide a reliable long-term investment for homeowners. We'll be happy to answer any questions or inquiries within 5 business days.

Here's an Overview of How It Works

Initial Consultation: Discovering Your Vision

After you contact Ishidoro Zen Gardens to arrange your free consultation, our team will promptly gather information regarding your specific preferences and lifestyle.

First Site Visit: Understanding Your Space

You and our team will choose a date and time to visit the site and get a clear idea of the available space for your project. Feel free to ask questions and express any concerns.

Conceptual Design: Crafting Your Vision

Next, our team will focus on developing practical diagrams and conceptual design blueprints for your personalized Japanese style gardens and landscapes.

Plan Review: Refining Your Dream Goals

You and our team will have another meeting to go over the plans and drawings, while we gather more information and hear your feedback on any specific expectations.

Design Approval: Turning Ideas into Reality

Once more, you and our team will meet to conduct a final assessment of the design, contract, warranties, payment arrangements, and official approval to move forward.

Project Timeline: Setting Expectations

Ishidoro Zen Gardens will provide you with estimated start and end dates; and very soon after that, your garden project will move into the production phase.

Continuous Communication: Every Step

From the commencement date, you will have unlimited access to all communication channels until the very end, when you do the final walkthrough and project inspection.

Inspection and Payment: Sweet Ownership

After your final inspection and approval, we will hand you the closing invoice and receive the balance payment. Thank you for referring Ishidoro Zen Gardens to your friends, and sharing a Google review.

Maintenance Estimate: Keeping It Beautiful

You will receive a free estimate for regular maintenance and care, without any obligations. This way, you can be sure that your gardens and landscapes will always be in their best condition.
